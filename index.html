A Breif Life History:

My Life:

I was born in La Mirada California. It is a suburb of Los Angeles.
I moved to a tiny desert town at a very young age called Helendale.
Helendale is in the middle of the Mojave desert in between Victoville
and Barstow California. At age 11 I then moved to Phoenix Arizona, huge
culture shock by the way. Within the next six years my family life would
take a turn for the worse. To keep my mind off all the problems going on
around me, I turned all my attention to tinkering. I would spend hours in my
room taking apart electronics to see how they worked. I would try making
different things out of them, although most of the time it never worked.
A few times I even went door to door to see if people had tv's or any other
type of electronics they didnt want. You would be amazed how much stuff you
could get this way. At 17 I moved to North carolina where I got my first real
job in the Corrigated Industry. That is when I knew I was born to work. I
loved it, the feeling to be proud of a hard day's work. From there I would move
on to try new type's of work.

A Brief Work History:

Work:

My first real job was in the Corrugated Industry making card board boxe's. I
absolutly loved it! Every day I would leave to go home after working long days,
I was always filled with pride that I did a good days work. I always knew that my
work produced quality, good looking product that the customer would be happy with.
I would move state to state approximatly every three years due to plant lay-off's,
and even closures. In that Industry lay-off's were extremly common. After several
years of it, I grew tired of the job uncertainty. I then decided to move onto
over the road commercial truck driving. I spent 10 years driving truck and found myself
yet again loving every minute of it. The sheer feeling of open freedom. No one telling
you what to do, no one telling you how to do it. It was completely up to me whether or
not I would be successfull or fail. In July 2017 I would experiance total computer failure
going down flagstaff mountain heading into Phoenix. It resulted in a roll-over that ended
my truck driving career after driving over 1.5 million miles accident free.
By now I reside in Illinois, and soon come across a companie called Rayovac.
I knew right away this was where I belonged. Totaly amazing co-wporkers, amazing
management. A complete package. I truly wanted to retire here. A few years after working there
the companie that owned Rayovac sold it to Energizer Holdings. Everyone knows who they are.
It only took Energizer one year to announce that they were closing Rayovac down, and moving
all there locations to a new plant in Franklin, Indiana. During my short time at Rayovac
I quickly went from the lowest production position to the highest. I was a Lead Packaging
Specialist. I was in charge of multiple lines and reported directly to top management.
My rapid rise was noticed by Corporate Energizer. They asked me to be part of a very small
plant start-up team in Indiana. I assisted in the new start-up for two years. Rayovac in Dixon,
Illinois officaly closed forever Dec. 31st 2021. That is what has led me to Coding Temple
To learn Computer coding, thanks to a few co-workers.
